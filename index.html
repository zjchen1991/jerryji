About

The Player

Jerry Ji is a talented Dutch professional golfer with a proven track record of success in both national and international competitions. After achieving multiple top placements in the Netherlands U18 rankings, he has continued to compete at the highest level. His dedication to the sport and continuous improvement make him one of the most promising young golfers from the Netherlands.

"Golf is a journey of constant learning. Every tournament is a chance to grow."
